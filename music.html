--- Music ---

When I was 13 I discovered GarageBand on my mother's mac. I started to play around with the different instruments and features and as one can imagine it sounded like an orchestra...

...An orchestra full of kindergarten kids.

While spending more and more time on trial and error and learning some stuff about the program here and there over the next few months, I quickly realized that the real professionals rather use Logic Pro X than GarageBand, so I quickly switched.
I then learned about basics of music production over the next few years and started trying to remix songs I liked. While doing so, I learned a lot about how to transform something that you have in your head into something that you can actually hear.
